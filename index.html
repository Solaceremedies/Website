About Us

Founded in 2007, Solace stands as a beacon of care and comfort, embodying its very name—a source of relief and reassurance. Guided by the belief that “Prevention is better than cure,” we are dedicated to fostering healthier communities through regular health awareness camps in collaboration with doctors and dieticians.
At Solace, we are committed to providing high-quality, cost-effective solutions in various medical fields, including general medicine, gynecology, general surgery, orthopedics, neurology, and diabetes care. With a focus on affordability and accessibility, our products are designed to empower both healthcare providers and patients, ensuring better health outcomes for all.
Together, we strive to make a difference—because your health and well-being are at the heart of everything we do.

We are dedicated to delivering innovative healthcare solutions to improve lives.
